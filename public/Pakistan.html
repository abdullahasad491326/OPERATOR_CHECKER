A Day of Pride

On this day, we honor the sacrifices of our heroes and celebrate the freedom and unity we cherish. The journey to independence was a testament to our strength and resilience.

Pakistan is not just a country, it's an emotion. We are all Pakistanis, this is our identity.

Facts About Independence

  • The Date: Pakistan was declared a sovereign state on August 14, 1947, a day before India.
  • The Capital: Karachi was the first capital of Pakistan. It was later moved to Islamabad in 1960.
  • The Founder: Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah was sworn in as the first Governor-General.
  • Ramadan 27: The night of August 14–15, 1947, coincided with the 27th of Ramadan, a highly sacred night in the Islamic calendar.
